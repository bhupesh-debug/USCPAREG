## Study Tips for Using This Cheat Sheet

1. **Cover the right column** — Say the mnemonic aloud and see if you can recall what each letter stands for before unchecking.
2. **Test yourself on the numbers table** — These specific figures are directly tested on MCQs. Flash-card every row.
3. **Focus on cross-topic mnemonics** — "PAID" appears in both S corp AND partnerships but means slightly different things. Know the difference.
4. **Prioritize the "FAST" and "MY LEGS"** mnemonics — Bankruptcy non-dischargeable debts and Statute of Frauds appear on nearly every REG exam.
5. **Day-before-exam strategy** — Read this entire sheet once, then close it and write down every mnemonic from memory. Any gaps = review that file.
